using dendrogram to find the optimal numbers of clusters¶

  • linkage matrix- is a matrix that capture the pairwise distance (or dissimilarities) between clusters of data points.
  • linkage methods- define how the distance between clusters is calculated when merging them.
  • ward's method -calculate the increase in the sum of squared distance from each data points to the centroid of its new cluster when tewo clusters are merged.it aims to minimise the variances within merged clusters.
